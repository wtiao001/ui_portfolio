inMuseum App Concept

inMuseum provides users an informative and engaging museum experience at the convenience of their mobile devices.

Team

Willis Tiao, Haley Strickland, Daniel Zahoul

Duration

2 weeks

Role

UX/UI Design

Software

Invision, Illustrator, Photoshop, XD

The Problem

Museums pique interest among the general public, from staunch enthusiests to those that are curious and want to learn more. With 55,000 museums around the world, it is impossible for the average person to visit even a fraction of offered exhibits. Not only are these museums missing out on potential customers, but also losing a chance to educate people on history, culture, and much more.
How can we bring an authentic museum experience to those that do not have the means to visit them?
